# VQMS Dashboard

This repository showcases a VQMS (Quality Management System) Dashboard developed in Power BI to provide centralized visibility across quality processes, compliance workflows, and end-to-end case tracking across GMP and Non-GMP operations.

**Note:** All screenshots are blurred to maintain confidentiality.

---

## 1. Overview

The VQMS Dashboard is designed to monitor and manage key quality processes, including Deviations, Change Controls, CAPAs, Lab Investigations, and related extensions. It provides a consolidated view of quality events across multiple departments to ensure better compliance tracking, faster decision-making, and improved operational transparency.

The solution includes 13 interactive pages covering GMP and Non-GMP workflows with dedicated tracking for lifecycle stages of quality events.

### Key Enhancements

1. **Centralized Quality Management View:**  
  Consolidates multiple quality systems (Deviations, CAPA, Change Control, Lab Investigations, Customer Complaints) into a single Power BI dashboard.
2. **GMP & Non-GMP Segmentation:**  
  Separate tracking and comparison of GMP vs Non-GMP processes for better compliance monitoring.

3. **End-to-End Workflow Tracking:**  
  Tracks complete lifecycle of quality events including Initiated, In Progress, Approved, Pending, Completed, and Cancelled statuses.

4. **CAPA & Change Control Visibility:**  
  Dedicated tracking for CAPA, CAPA Extensions, Change Control, and Change Action items with full status breakdown.

5. **Lab Investigation Monitoring:**  
  Provides visibility into lab investigations including progress, resolution status, and pending actions.

6 **Consolidated KPI Page (Single View):**  
  A unified page displaying ~45 KPIs across:
  - CAPA & CAPA Actions  
  - Change Actions  
  - Deviations  
  - Customer Complaints  
  - Change Controls  
  - Lab Investigations  
  - CAPA Extensions  

7. **Operational Status Insights:**  
  Clear breakdown of cases by:
  - Resolved  
  - In Progress  
  - Pending  
  - Cancelled  
  - Initiated  
  - Approved  

8. **User & Department Consolidation:**  
  Visibility across users, departments, and ownership to track accountability and workload distribution.
